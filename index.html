BUSINESS MANAGEMENT:

Business management is the process of planning, organizing, directing, and controlling the activities of a business or organization to achieve its goals and objectives.

RISK MANAGEMENT:

Risk management is the process of identifying, assessing and controlling financial, legal, strategic and security risks to an organization's capital and earnings.
